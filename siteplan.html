Site Plan

The Reading Corner

Site Name

The Reading Corner

I chose this name because it is warm and inviting, suggesting a cozy place for people to share their love of reading. It's a simple, memorable name that accurately reflects the site's purpose.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a digital hub for a reading club community. The site will provide a catalog of all the books the club has read, information about upcoming events and meetings, and a point of contact for new members to join.

Scenarios

Here are two scenarios a site visitor might have:

  • Scenario 1: I'm a new reader looking to join a club. How can I find information about the club and sign up?
  • Scenario 2: I'm a club member looking for the title of the book we read two months ago. Where can I find a list of all the books the club has read?

Typography

The site will use the following fonts:

  • Heading Font:`Lora`- A classic serif font that will be used for main titles.
  • Body Font:`Poppins`- A clean, modern sans-serif font that will be used for body text and navigation.
